Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly oil a 9Ã—13 baking dish or coat with nonstick spray.
  2. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine ricotta, 1/2 cup mozzarella and egg; set aside.
  4. Place into oven and bake for 35-45 minutes, or until bubbling. Then broil for 2-3 minutes, or until top is browned in spots.
  5. Let cool 15 minutes. Serve, garnished with parsley, if desired.
